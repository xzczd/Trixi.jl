Authors

Trixi's development is coordinated by a group of principal developers, who are also its main contributors and who can be contacted in case of questions about Trixi. In addition, there are contributors who have provided substantial additions or modifications. Together, these two groups form "The Trixi Authors" as mentioned under License.

Principal Developers

  • Michael Schlottke-Lakemper, University of Cologne, Germany
  • Gregor Gassner, University of Cologne, Germany
  • Hendrik Ranocha, University of Münster, Germany
  • Andrew Winters, Linköping University, Sweden
